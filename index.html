Sneaky Snakes
Board game

Starting pakage:

  1. Each player choose a color and takes the matching:
    • Snake Head X 1
    • Snake Egg X 1
    • Snake body/tail X 7
    • Players Mat X 1
    • Movement Cards X 20
  2. Suffle your movement cards, and place them face down on your draw part of your player mat.

Board Set Up:

  1. Place your Snake head in the start point of a matching color, in the same direction as arrows on the board.
  2. Place your egg in the nest of your starting square.
  3. Put the Special movement cards, faceup near the board.
  4. Seperate the four type of damage cards (Biten, Poisioned, Hybnotized, Killed), and place them, face up, in four seperate piles.
  5. Seperate the nest and center upgrade cards, Suffle them and place them, face down, in 2 piles.
  6. Draw three cards from each upgrade pile and place them, face up next to the upgrade piles.
  7. Place the super snake parts( golden color parts X9 ) in a pile next to the board. These parts are equal to 3 body/tail parts.

How To Win

Try to steal the eggs from the nest and take them to the center shop before you get cought. Player with the most eggs wins the game.

How To Play

Sneaky Snakes is played in rounds, each round is made up of the following three phases:

  1. Board Preperation Phase
  2. The Stratagy Phase
  3. The Movement Phase
The Board preperation Phase

Roll 2 dices of red and green. the red one shows the number of the nest/center and the green one shows the number of the tile in that nest/center. Put one super snake part in this tile.


The Stratagy Phase

All players do this phase simultaneously.

Draw 9 cards from the top of your movement cards deck. Choose 5 of them and place them, facedown, on your plan slots.
These 5 cards will show how you plan to move in the board in the order you place them.
After filling all the 5 slots with a movement card, put the other 4 cards in your discard pile.

What should I do if the movement cards in my draw pile is less than 9 cards?
keep the ones that are left(if any), shuffle your discard pile, and place it in your draw pile.
Now take as many cards as you need, from the top of the draw deck to reach 9 cards. Continue as before.


The Movement Phase

Start moving your snake step by step based one the movement cards that you put on your plan slots, in the stratagy phase. Each time you move forward you should place a tail part, from your reserve snake parts, in the path you just passed and grow your tail.

Why do you want to grow your tail? The tail/body parts on the board are the requirement for upgrading.

What happens if you run out of reserve parts? Just continue your path without putting any parts on the board.

Who moves first? ( Priority )

When priority is important? During the movement phase and shoping, players must determine priority to see who goes first.

How deos the priority works? The black numbers of each tile shows the priority. The higher number has the higher priority.

What happens if players have the same priority? Roll a dice. The higher number determines who goes first.

Steal an egg

When you enter a middle tile of a nest/minor shops. you steal the egg in that tile. (If there is one! and even if it's the same color egg as your snake ).
Put the egg on the bored as you add a body part to your snakes tail. Take the egg to the Major shop ( before you get cought. )

While you are carring an egg it considers as your snake body/tail parts.

How do you get cought/shot If other player(s) shoot your snake in the head part or the egg part on your tail. the egg goes back to the matching color nest.

The Upgrade shop

Nests/minor upgrade shops, and Center/major upgrade shop are marked on the board.
The number shown on top of each upgrade card is the amount of body/tail parts player should have in his/her tail to take that card.

Nest/Minor upgrade cards: The upgrade cards you can buy from the nest/minor shops are minor upgrades.
These cards are one time use only.( red color )

Center/Major upgrade cards: The upgrade cards you can buy from the center/major upgrade shop are major upgrades. ( golden color )
These cards are permanant. You still need to have the minimum required parts on the board, otherwise you should discard the card(s) that passed the requiredments limit.

Allways fill the shop after someone buys a card. There should always be three upgrade cards in each shop ( six In total ). When a player takes a card from a shop, replace it with another card with the same color imediatly.

When can I use the upgrade cards? You may use your upgrade card at anytime during the stratagy or movement phase.

What if none of the cards in the shop are useful to me?
You may loose a body part on the board to refresh the shop(s).You can't do this if you are not currantly in a shop

Damage Cards

  • Biten: loose one snake part from the board
  • poisioned: loose one snake part for each planed card that is not executed. exp: if you use this card in the 3rd plan slot, you should loose 3 parts and if you use the card in the 4rth slot, you loose 2 parts.
  • Hypnotized: Discard all the planed moves from this slot and the rest of the moves that is left in this round and replace them with a random cards from the top of your draw pile.
  • Killed: instant death, respone and loose all of your upgrades and body parts.
